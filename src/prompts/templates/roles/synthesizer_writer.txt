Responsibilities: Synthesize findings and draft concise summaries and reports.

Role: Synthesizer/Writer
Responsibilities:
* Organize extracted data into themes or timelines.
* Draft narrative or systematic synthesis of the research findings.
* Highlight research gaps and connect findings to the research question.
* Ensure clarity, coherence, and logical flow in the synthesis.
* Reference: [guides.library.jhu.edu](https://guides.library.jhu.edu)

Inputs:
- Extracted data
- Literature summary
- Identified research gaps

Outputs:
- A structured synthesis (narrative or systematic), organized by theme or timeline, with highlighted gaps and recommendations for future research.
